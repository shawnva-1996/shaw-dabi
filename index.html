New in Singapore? 5 Things Every Filipino Must Know

Just arrived and getting settled? Navigating a new country can be overwhelming. Here are five essential tips to help you manage your finances and make the most of your new life in Singapore, right from the start.

  • Mastering Remittance: Don't just go to the first remittance center you see. I'll show you how to compare rates and fees to ensure more of your hard-earned money makes it home.
  • Grocery Hacks for the Savvy Saver: Learn where the locals and long-time expats shop to save significantly on your weekly grocery bills without sacrificing quality.
  • A Taste of Home (Without Breaking the Bank): Craving authentic Filipino food? I know the best spots like J&J Food Hub and Lucy's Kitchen that offer delicious meals at great prices.
  • Understanding Your Employment Pass / Work Permit: What are your rights? What deductions are legal? Knowing the basics can save you from major headaches down the road.
  • Building Your 'Uwi-an' Fund from Day 1: "Uwi-an" (going home for good) planning starts now. I'll share a simple strategy to start saving, no matter how small, for your future in the Philippines.
